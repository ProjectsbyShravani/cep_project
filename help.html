Frequently Asked Questions

How do I apply for a job?

To apply for a job, you can visit the "Job Board" section, select a job, and follow the application process.

What is the Analytics section?

The Analytics section provides insights into job trends, skills demand, and other key data to help you in your career planning.

How can I change my settings?

You can update your profile, notification preferences, and privacy settings from the "Settings" section in the dashboard.
